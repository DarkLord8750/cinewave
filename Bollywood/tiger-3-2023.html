Tiger 3 is a 2023 Indian action-thriller movie. It is the sequel to Ek Tha Tiger (2012) and Tiger Zinda Hai (2015). The movie stars Salman Khan, Katrina Kaif, and Emraan Hashmi. In the movie, Tiger (Salman Khan) and his partner Zoya (Katrina Kaif) must stop a rogue ISI agent, Aatish Rehman (Emraan Hashmi), from staging a coup against the Government of Pakistan. Aatish issues an ultimatum to Tiger and Zoya: comply with his demands or risk losing their son to a brutal fate.
